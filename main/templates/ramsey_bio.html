The Beginning

April 5, 2021

On April 5th 2021, Ramsey (originally named "Dragonfly" :/) was born on a farm near Shiloh, TN. She had many other australian brothers and sisters that she were probably frolicking around with in her first days. She also got her tail chopped off at the age of 1 day old, but im pretty sure she didn't even notice. Above is the first know picture of her existence.

Meeting the Family

June 2021

I adopted Ramsey at the age of 8 weeks, where we spent a few weeks in Collierville,TN in my parents' home. She was met there by our other two dogs, Cici (golden doodle) and Bear (nobody knows). As shown, Ramsey and Cici almost immediately bonded, while Bear mostly just tried to ignore whatever was happening.

Boston Adventures

July 2021

From Tennessee, Ramsey set out for Boston, MA where she would have to take her first plane ride at the age of less than 3 months old. Once she arrived, she settled in quickly and would spend her afternoons exploring the Boston Commons. She became such a spectacle in the neighborhood, that it was a common for her to draw larger crowds of tourists than the nearby historical landmarks.

The Incident

April 2023

During her time at Auburn, Ramsey experienced a significant event that would change her perspective on college life. This incident taught her valuable lessons about resilience and adaptability, shaping her into the mature dog she would become. The experience strengthened her bond with her family and prepared her for the next chapter of her adventure.

California Bound

June 2022 - April 2025

The big move to California marked a new chapter in Ramsey's life. Settling in Menlo Park, she discovered the joys of West Coast living - from hiking trails to dog-friendly cafes. The mild climate and outdoor lifestyle suited her perfectly, and she quickly adapted to the tech hub atmosphere, becoming a familiar face in the neighborhood.

Beach Vacation

May 2025

Ramsey's first real beach vacation in Montara was a revelation. She discovered her love for sand between her toes and the endless entertainment of chasing waves. The coastal air and ocean breeze became her new favorite elements, and she spent hours exploring tide pools and making friends with fellow beach-going dogs.

City Pup Pt. 2

June 2025 - Present

Moving to San Francisco brought Ramsey full circle back to city life, but this time as a seasoned urban explorer. The hills of SF provided new challenges and adventures, from navigating steep streets to discovering hidden dog parks. She's become a true San Francisco native, complete with a sophisticated palate for artisanal dog treats.
