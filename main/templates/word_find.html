How to Play
Objective

Form as many valid words as possible by connecting adjacent letters on the grid before time runs out!

How to Form Words
  • Click letters to select them in sequence
  • Connect adjacent letters - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • Minimum 3 letters required to form a valid word
  • Click a selected letter to deselect it and all letters after it
  • Submit your word using the Submit button
Scoring System
3 letters: 1 point
4 letters: 2 points
5 letters: 4 points
6 letters: 6 points
7 letters: 8 points
8+ letters: 11 points
Game Rules
  • You have 90 seconds to find as many words as possible
  • Each word can only be used once per game
  • Words must be valid English words
  • Letters must be connected in sequence (no skipping)
  • Your final score determines your leaderboard position
